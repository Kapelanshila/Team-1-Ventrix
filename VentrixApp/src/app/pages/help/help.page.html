The app consists of a sliding feature that allows you to move an order to the next process.



Collect an order then proceed to delivery in which you can View, Schedule, Edit, Change the status to En-Route and complete a particular order

[There are different button options for diffrent functionalities]

Click on the order and use a sliding motion (drag) to the left or right to reveal the buttons



The status of an order will be updated and displayed throughout the process of an order on the App
